Coffee

Coffee is darkly colored, bitter, slightly acidic and has a stimulating effect in humans, primarily due to its caffeine content. It is one of the most popular drinks in the world and can be prepared and presented in a variety of ways

Ingredients

  • Coffee beans
  • Water
  • Sugar

Preperation

  1. Place a cup of water on stove to boil
  2. Grind the coffe beans
  3. Put that coffe powder into water
  4. Wait until the water boils
  5. Then pour it into a cup
  6. Finally add some sugar
  7. Here you go your coffe is ready
